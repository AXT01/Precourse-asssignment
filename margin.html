CHROME

Google Chrome is a Google-designed free Web browser that is used to view Internet sites. As of May 2020, with more than 60 percent of web browser market share, it’s the world’s most popular web browser option. Google Chrome is also a cross-platform browser, so versions are running on many laptops, handheld devices, and Operating systems.
